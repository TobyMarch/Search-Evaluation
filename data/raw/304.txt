<p><i><b>The

the
Witch
and

Wardrobe</b></i>,
often
shortened
to
<b>LWW</b>,
was
written
by
<a
href="/wiki/C._S._Lewis"
title="C.
S.
Lewis">C.
S.
Lewis
and</a>
published
in
1950.
It
presented
the
story
of
four
ordinary
English
children
-
<a
href="/wiki/Peter_Pevensie"
title="Peter
Pevensie">Peter</a>,
<a
href="/wiki/Susan_Pevensie"
title="Susan
Pevensie">Susan</a>,

<p>Because

World

old
<a

<a

to

back

the
wardrobe,

that
the
White
Witch
would

</p>







<p>Later,

on

&quot;imaginary&quot;

into

wardrobe

Lucy

Peter
that
they
needed
to
help
her
new

Mr.
Tumnus,
who
was
arrested.
