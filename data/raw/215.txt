<h2
class="rm">Biography</h2>










<h3
class="remove-top-margin">Cambridge,
England,
United
Kingdom
(<span
itemprop="foundingDate">1965</span>
–
1994)</h3>









</hgroup>








</header>








<div
class="wiki">









<div
id="wikiAbstract"
class="wiki-abstract">










<div
class="wiki-text">











Pink
Floyd
was
a











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/psychedelic"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">psychedelic</a>/










<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/progressive%20rock"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">progressive
rock</a>
band
formed
in
Cambridge,
England,
United
Kingdom
in
1965.
Pink
Floyd
is
one
of











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/rock"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">rock</a>’s
most
successful
and
influential
acts,
having
sold
over
200
million
albums
worldwide
and
with
74.5
million
certified
units
in
the
United
States,
making
them
one
of
the
best-selling
artists
of
all
time.











<br
/>











<br
/>
The
band’s
classic
lineup
was











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Roger+Waters"
class="bbcode_artist">Roger
Waters</a>
(vocals,
bass),











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/David+Gilmour"
class="bbcode_artist">David
Gilmour</a>
(vocals,
guitar),











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Rick+Wright"
class="bbcode_artist">Rick
Wright</a>
(organ,
keyboards,
vocals)
and











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Nick+Mason"
class="bbcode_artist">Nick
Mason</a>
(drums).











<span
class="hide">
Gilmour
was
brought
into
the
band
in
1968
to
replace
the
band’s
founder,
singer,
guitarist
and
songwriter
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Syd+Barrett"
class="bbcode_artist">Syd
Barrett</a>,
who
had
become
increasingly
erratic
and
departed
from
the
band
a
few
months
after
Gilmour’s
addition.
The
band
became
known
for
their
advancements
in
the
genres
of
psychedelic
rock
and
progressive
rock
music,
philosophical
lyrics,
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/avant-garde"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">avant-garde</a>
compositions,
sonic
experimentation,
innovative
cover
art
and
elaborate
live
shows.
<br
/>
<br
/>
Pink
Floyd
enjoyed
modest
success
in
the
late-1960s
as
a
psychedelic
band
led
by
Syd
Barrett.
Barrett’s
increasingly
erratic
behavior
eventually
caused
his
colleagues
to
replace
him
after
<a
title="Pink
Floyd
-
The
Piper
at
the
Gates
of
Dawn"
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Pink+Floyd/The+Piper+at+the+Gates+of+Dawn"
class="bbcode_album">The
Piper
at
the
Gates
of
Dawn</a>
with
guitarist
David
Gilmour.
The
band
went
on
to
record
several
elaborate
concept
albums;
achieving
worldwide
success
with
1973’s
<a
title="Pink
Floyd
-
The
Dark
Side
of
the
Moon"
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Pink+Floyd/The+Dark+Side+of+the+Moon"
class="bbcode_album">The
Dark
Side
of
the
Moon</a>
(The
second
best-selling
album
of
all
time),
1975’s
<a
title="Pink
Floyd
-
Wish
You
Were
Here"
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Pink+Floyd/Wish+You+Were+Here"
class="bbcode_album">Wish
You
Were
Here</a>,
1977’s
<a
title="Pink
Floyd
-
Animals"
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Pink+Floyd/Animals"
class="bbcode_album">Animals</a>,
and
1979’s
<a
title="Pink
Floyd
-
The
Wall"
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Pink+Floyd/The+Wall"
class="bbcode_album">The
Wall</a>,
among
the
best-selling,
most
critically
acclaimed,
and
enduringly
popular
albums
in
rock
music
history.
</span>
