<!--paging_filter-->Celine
is
the
world's
best-selling
solo
artist
and
a
five-time
Grammy
Award
winner.
She
has
sold
more
than
100
million
albums
in
the
past
decade.
'All
The
Way
-
A
Decade
Of
Song'
is
a
collection
of
Celine's
greatest
hits
in
addition
to
seven
new
songs
recorded
specifically
for
this
collection.













<br
/>













<br
/>
The
title
track
&quot;All
The
Way&quot;
is
a
loving
tribute
to
one
of
Celine's
all-time
favorite
singers,
Frank
Sinatra.
&quot;That's
The
Way
It
Is&quot;
is
the
premiere
single
and
video
from
the
album,
produced
by
Max
Martin
and
Kristian
Lundin
(Backstreet
Boys,
Britney
Spears).
&quot;That's
The
Way
It
Is&quot;
is
a
heartfelt
mid-tempo
track
perfectly
in
tune
with
contemporary
pop
music.
Another
classic
track
is
&quot;The
First
Time
Ever
I
Saw
Your
Face&quot;
an
Ewen
MacColl
ballad
which
became
a
No.
1
hit
for
Roberta
Flack
in
1972.
Produced
by
longtime
collaborator
David
Foster,
this
sensitive
production
and
orchestration
frame
one
of
Celine's
finest
vocal
performances.
Celine
also
worked
with
Robert
John
&quot;Mutt&quot;
Lange,
best
known
for
his
multi-platinum
recordings
with
Shania
Twain,
on
&quot;If
Walls
Could
Talk.&quot;
