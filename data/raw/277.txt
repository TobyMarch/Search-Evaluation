<h2><span

id="Production">Production</span></h2>








<p>The
film
was
shot
on
location
in
Tunisia.</p>








<p>In
1973,
Lerner
and
Loewe
recorded
the
score
at
the
<a
href="/wiki/Palm_Springs_Desert_Museum?qsrc=3044"
title="Palm
Springs
Desert
Museum">Palm
Springs
Desert
Museum</a>,
with
Lerner
on
vocals
and
Loewe
at
the
piano.
It
included
&quot;Matters
of
Consequence&quot;,
which
was
cut
from
the
film.
It
is
one
of
only
a
few
existing
recordings
of
the
duo
performing
together.</p>








<p>The
film
had
production
design
by
<a
href="/wiki/John_Barry_(set_designer)?qsrc=3044"
title="John
Barry
(set
designer)">John
Barry</a>
(not
to
be
confused
with
composer
<a

Barry
(composer)">John
Barry</a>,

later

a

musical-adaptation
of

theatre">Broadway</a>).</p>
