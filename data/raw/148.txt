Elvis
Aaron

(Tupelo,

1935

A
cultural
icon,
he
is
widely
known

single
name
Elvis.











<br
/>











<br
/>
Elvis
Presley

<a

<a

class="bbcode_artist">Sam
Philips</a>
recorded
Elvis
performing
several
of
Elvis’
favourite
songs
with











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Scotty+Moore"
class="bbcode_artist">Scotty
Moore</a>
(guitar)
and











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Bill+Black"
class="bbcode_artist">Bill
Black</a>
(bass).
One
of
these
covers
was
a
country
and
western
song,
“










<a
class="previewbutton
preview-track"

/></a>










<a

class="bbcode_track">Blue

<br

Elvis
is

189.2

<span
class="hide">
<br

Before
<a

<a

class="bbcode_label">rca

they

six
two-sided

<a

song

<a

One

ability
to
merge
attributes
of

“white”
(<span
title="Unknown

hit

“<a
class="previewbutton

src="http://cdn.last.fm/flatness/clear.gif"
style=""
/></a><a
title="Elvis
Presley
–
Heartbreak
Hotel"
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Elvis+Presley/_/Heartbreak+Hotel"
class="bbcode_track">Heartbreak
Hotel</a>”
which
reached
#1
not
only
in
the
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/pop"
class="bbcode_tag"

charts,
but
the
R&amp;B

<span

<br
/>
<br
/>
Elvis
remains

His

only
