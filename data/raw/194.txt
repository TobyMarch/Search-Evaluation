<p>Often

band’s

song

fans

“Kashmir”

epic.
The
riff

Robert

<p>The

all
of
rock
music.
“Stairway
To
Heaven”
is
both
the
most
beloved
and

song
ever
written.
For
some
the
song
is
the
most
beautiful
and
moving
piece
of
music
they’ve
ever
heard,
while
others
cringe
at
the

of
hearing
it.
The

it

necessarily
the
best
song
ever
written,
it’s
up
there.
The
lyrics
are
brilliant,
referring
both
to
the
greed
of
people
while
taking
an
optimistic
perspective.
Oh,
and
that
guitar
solo
is
pretty
good
too.</p>

<p>A

bad
which

<p>“When

Breaks”

The

mis-credited

cover
of

Bonham,

used

decades,

by

Boys.</p>
